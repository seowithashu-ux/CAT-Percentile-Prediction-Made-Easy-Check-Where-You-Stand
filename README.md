## Why Percentile Prediction Matters
For thousands of aspirants appearing in CAT each year, the actual score doesn’t tell the full story — your percentile does. Percentile reflects how you performed relative to all other test-takers, not just how many marks you scored. That’s why a raw mark of 90 today could mean a top percentile or a mid-range one, depending on difficulty levels, slot challenge, and overall candidate performance.
